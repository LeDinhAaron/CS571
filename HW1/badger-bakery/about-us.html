About us!

Welcome to Badger Bakery, where we transform ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences through the art of baking. Nestled within the vibrant campus of the University of Wisconsin - Madison, Badger Bakery is more than just a place to sate your sweet tooth; it's a destination for culinary exploration, nostalgia, and community.

Our Story

Founded by a group of passionate Badgers who share a deep love for the art of baking, Badger Bakery was born out of a desire to bring a taste of home to the vibrant community of UW-Madison. Our journey began with a simple idea: to infuse the spirit of Wisconsin into every bite, delivering a slice of comfort and nostalgia with every bite.

Our Baked Delights

At Badger Bakery, we believe that the finest ingredients create the most memorable flavors. We source locally whenever possible, ensuring that our treats are not only mouthwateringly delicious but also support the Wisconsin farming community. From classic chocolate chip cookies that remind you of home to innovative creations that surprise and delight, our menu offers a wide range of baked goods to satisfy every craving.

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is the cornerstone of our bakery. Each morning, our dedicated bakers rise early to handcraft every item on our menu. From kneading dough to frosting cupcakes, we pour our hearts into every step of the baking process. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every product that leaves our ovens is of the highest quality and taste.

Campus and Community

Located near the heart of the UW-Madison campus, Badger Bakery has become a cherished part of the community. Whether you're a student looking for a quick study break snack, a faculty member seeking a sweet treat, or a visitor wanting to experience the warmth of Badger hospitality, our doors are always open.

Customer Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our ultimate goal. We love to see the smiles our treats bring to your faces, and your feedback helps us continually improve. We take pride in being a part of your Badger experience and look forward to serving you for many years to come.

Visit Us Today

Stop by Badger Bakery today and experience the taste of Wisconsin with a side of Badger pride. Whether you're enjoying a warm cookie after a long day of classes or picking up a special cake for a celebration, we're here to make your day a little sweeter. Thank you for choosing Badger Bakery, and with warm regards, go Badgers!
